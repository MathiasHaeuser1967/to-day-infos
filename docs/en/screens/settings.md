# Settings

Configure how ToDay behaves.

## General
- **Theme**: light/dark/system
- **Language**: system language

## Notifications & alarms
- **Allow notifications**
- **Exact alarms** (if supported)
- **Reminder channels** (sound/vibration)
- **Snooze defaults**

## Speech (TTS)
- **Hourly announcements** (on/off)
- **Voice & rate**
- **Speak only when screen is on**

## Power management
- Disable **battery optimization** for ToDay (**No restrictions**) to avoid delays.

## Data
- **Backup/restore** (device-dependent)
